YesTeachers — Analogy Generator
ConceptHow DNA replication works
AudienceGrade 10 · Keep it relatable
💡 DNA Replication — Analogy
🎯 Main analogy
Think of DNA replication like photocopying a recipe book. The copier (helicase) unzips the book down the spine, then two scribes (DNA polymerase) each copy one half — so you end up with two identical cookbooks from one original.
🔗 Concept mapping
Helicase = the person unzipping the book's spine
DNA polymerase = the scribe copying each page
Base pairs = the matching words on each page
Two daughter strands = two complete cookbooks

Relatable to everyday experience · Preserves the logic · Easy to extend
