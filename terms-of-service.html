02 Description of Service

YesTeachers is an AI-powered educational content generation platform designed exclusively for teachers and educational professionals. The Service enables users to produce classroom-ready materials in seconds using artificial intelligence.

Core tools included

  • Lesson Planner — structured lesson plans with objectives, timed flow, and differentiation strategies
  • Lesson Generator — full written lesson content including hook, explanation, examples, and discussion questions
  • Quiz Generator — multiple choice and short-answer quizzes with complete answer keys and formula sheets
  • Diagram Generator — labelled educational SVG diagrams, print-ready
  • Essay Feedback — inline markup, rubric scoring, and model answer excerpts
  • Vocabulary Builder — topic word lists with definitions, example sentences, and difficulty ratings
  • Answer Grader — structured score and feedback on student answers
  • Analogy Generator — concept explanations through multiple relatable analogies

Additional features include curriculum/standard alignment, source material upload (Pro), preset saving, PDF export, multi-language output, and a cloud-based dashboard accessible from any device.

The Service is provided via a web application at yesteachers.com and accessed through any modern web browser. No software installation is required.
