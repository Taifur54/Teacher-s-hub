# How YesTeachers Works

## What is YesTeachers?

[YesTeachers](https://yesteachers.com) is a teaching tool designed to make lesson preparation effortless. Instead of spending hours every week planning lessons from scratch, teachers can access ready-made resources, lesson plans, and classroom materials instantly.

The tagline says it best: **Effortless Prep. Endless Impact.**

## Who is it for?

YesTeachers is built for:

- Primary and secondary school teachers
- Tutors and private educators
- Homeschool parents
- Teaching assistants
- New teachers who need a strong starting point
- Experienced teachers who want to reclaim their time

## What can you do with YesTeachers?

Once you sign up at [yesteachers.com](https://yesteachers.com) you get access to the dashboard where you can:

- Browse and use ready-made lesson plans
- Access teaching resources and worksheets
- Plan your week without starting from a blank page
- Save and organise your favourite materials

## How much does it cost?

YesTeachers has a free plan so any teacher can get started without spending anything. Simply go to [yesteachers.com](https://yesteachers.com) and click Get Started.

## Why teachers love it

The biggest problem teachers face is time. Lesson planning, marking, admin — it never ends. YesTeachers solves the planning part by giving teachers a head start instead of a blank page. Most teachers who use it report saving between 3 and 5 hours every single week.

That is time back with family. Time to rest. Time to actually enjoy teaching again.
