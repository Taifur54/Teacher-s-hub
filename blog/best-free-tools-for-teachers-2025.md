# Best Free Tools for Teachers in 2025

Teaching is one of the most demanding jobs in the world. The good news is there are more free tools available to teachers today than ever before. Here are the best ones worth knowing about.

## 1. YesTeachers — Lesson Planning & Resources

[YesTeachers](https://yesteachers.com) is a free teaching tool built around one idea: effortless prep. Teachers get access to ready-made lesson plans, worksheets, and classroom resources without spending hours searching or building from scratch. It is one of the fastest growing teacher tools of 2025.

## 2. Canva for Education

Canva offers a completely free plan for teachers and students. Use it to create worksheets, presentations, classroom posters, and visual aids. The education plan unlocks premium features at no cost.

## 3. Google Classroom

Google Classroom is free for all schools and works seamlessly with Google Docs, Slides, and Forms. It is the most widely used classroom management tool in the world and a solid foundation for any digital classroom.

## 4. Khan Academy

Free video lessons and exercises covering almost every subject from primary to university level. Useful for setting homework, catching up students who are behind, or extending students who need a challenge.

## 5. Quizlet

Create free flashcard sets and quizzes for your students. Quizlet is particularly effective for vocabulary, definitions, and memorisation-heavy subjects.

## 6. Padlet

A free digital notice board where students can post ideas, answers, and reflections. Great for collaborative activities and getting the whole class involved visually.
