01 Introduction

YesTeachers (sole proprietor, operating at yesteachers.com) is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and your rights.

By using the Service, you consent to the practices described in this policy. This policy applies to all users of yesteachers.com and the YesTeachers dashboard.

We do not sell your personal data. We do not use your classroom content or tool inputs to train AI models. Your data is used solely to provide and improve the Service.

03 How We Use Your Information

  • Provide the Service — process tool requests, generate AI outputs, manage your account and subscription
  • Personalise your experience — remember profile, language preference, and saved presets
  • Process payments — manage subscriptions, billing cycles, and trial activations via Paddle
  • Communicate with you — account notifications, support responses, service updates
  • Improve the Service — analyse aggregated, anonymised usage patterns to fix bugs and build features
  • Ensure security — detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorised access
  • Legal compliance — respond to lawful requests from authorities where required

We do not use your data for advertising targeting, behavioural profiling, or sale to third parties.

05 AI Processing & Your Content

When you use a YesTeachers tool, the content you submit is sent to Anthropic's API to generate a response. This is essential to the function of the Service.

We do not use your tool inputs, classroom content, or student data to train AI models. Anthropic's API terms also prohibit using API inputs for model training by default.

We recommend you do not include personally identifiable student information (full names, ID numbers, contact details) in tool inputs. Use anonymised or example names when testing with student work.

AI-generated outputs are session-based and are not permanently stored on our servers unless you explicitly save them through a future save feature.

06 Paddle & Payment Data

Payments are processed by Paddle.com Market Limited, who acts as our Merchant of Record. When you subscribe, Paddle collects and processes your payment information (card details, billing address) directly. YesTeachers does not store your card number or CVV.

Paddle's handling of your payment data is governed by Paddle's Privacy Policy. We receive only transaction confirmation and subscription status from Paddle.

For bKash payments, your bKash number and transaction reference are shared with us and bKash to process and confirm payment. bKash's own privacy policy applies to their handling of your data.

07 Cookies & Local Storage

We use minimal cookies and browser local storage to operate the Service:

  • Authentication cookies — keep you logged in (essential, required for the Service to function)
  • Preference storage (localStorage) — remember your language selection, UI preferences, and saved presets
  • Basic analytics — aggregated usage data to understand feature adoption (not tied to individual identity)

We do not use advertising cookies or cross-site tracking. You can clear cookies and local storage via your browser settings, which will log you out and reset your preferences.

08 Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on Supabase infrastructure. We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • HTTPS (TLS) encryption for all data in transit
  • Encrypted storage for sensitive data at rest
  • Row-level security (RLS) policies so users can only access their own data
  • Hashed passwords — your password is never stored in readable form

In the event of a data breach materially affecting your personal information, we will notify you as required by applicable law.

09 Data Retention

  • Account data — retained while your account is active and for up to 90 days after deletion to allow recovery
  • Billing records — retained for 7 years for financial compliance
  • Usage logs — retained for up to 12 months
  • Tool inputs / generated outputs — session-only; not permanently stored

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by emailing us.

10 Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion — request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability — request your data in a portable format
  • Objection — object to certain processing activities

To exercise any of these rights, email support@yesteachers.com. We will respond within 30 days. You can update your profile information directly in your dashboard at any time.

11 Children's Privacy

YesTeachers is designed for teachers and educational professionals aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. Teachers should not enter personally identifiable student data (names, ID numbers) into tool inputs — anonymise student work before submission.

If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a child under 13, contact us immediately and we will delete it promptly.

12 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting a notice on our website or emailing your registered address. Continued use of the Service after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
