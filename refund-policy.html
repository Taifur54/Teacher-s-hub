01 Overview

At YesTeachers, we want you to be completely satisfied with your subscription. This Refund Policy explains when refunds are available, how to request one, and how they are processed.

We offer a free trial so you can experience the Service before purchasing. We encourage you to use the trial to evaluate whether YesTeachers meets your needs before subscribing.

We offer a 7-day refund window from the date of your first paid charge. If you are not satisfied, contact us within 7 days and we will issue a full refund — no questions asked.

02 Free Trial

YesTeachers may offer a free trial period to new subscribers. During the trial:

  • You have full access to the features included in your selected plan
  • No payment is charged until the trial period ends
  • You may cancel before the trial ends to avoid being charged
  • Trial periods are available once per user — creating multiple accounts to extend a trial is a violation of our Terms of Service

Promo codes may be used to activate a free trial at signup or via your profile dashboard. Each promo code is single-use and cannot be transferred.

03 Standard Refund Policy

7-day satisfaction guarantee (first charge only)

If you subscribe to a paid plan for the first time and are not satisfied, you may request a full refund within 7 days of the initial charge. This applies to your first payment only.

To qualify:

  • Your refund request must be submitted within 7 days of the charge date
  • This applies to first-time subscriptions only — not renewals
  • Your account must not have violated our Terms of Service

Monthly renewals

For recurring monthly charges after the first payment, we generally do not offer refunds for partial months. However, we will consider refund requests in exceptional circumstances, including:

  • Significant technical failures that prevented you from accessing the Service for an extended period
  • Duplicate charges due to a billing error on our side
  • Charges made after a cancellation was properly submitted

We do not offer refunds for: forgetting to cancel before renewal, dissatisfaction with AI-generated outputs in general, or change of mind after more than 7 days of your first payment.

04 Cancellation Policy

You can cancel your subscription at any time. Here is what happens when you cancel:

  • Your subscription remains active until the end of your current billing period
  • You will not be charged at the next renewal date
  • You retain access to all paid features until the period expires
  • Your account and saved data remain accessible — we do not delete your account on cancellation

To cancel, contact us at support@yesteachers.com or use the cancellation option in your account settings.

Cancel before your next billing date and you will not be charged. There are no cancellation fees.

05 How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, follow these steps:

1

Email us

Send an email to support@yesteachers.com with the subject line "Refund Request".

2

Include your details

Include your registered email address, the date of the charge, and a brief reason for your request. This helps us process it faster.

3

We review and respond

We aim to respond to all refund requests within 2 business days. We will let you know the outcome and next steps.

4

Refund processed

Approved refunds are processed back to your original payment method. Processing time depends on your payment provider — typically 5–10 business days for cards, and varies for bKash.

06 Payment Method Notes

Credit / Debit cards (via Paddle)

Refunds are processed back to the original card. Depending on your bank, it may take 5–10 business days to appear on your statement.

bKash

Refunds for bKash payments are processed back to the original bKash number. Processing typically takes 3–7 business days. We will confirm the refund via email once initiated.

Note: bKash transaction fees charged by bKash at the time of payment are non-refundable as they are collected by bKash, not by YesTeachers.

07 Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will not be issued in the following circumstances:

  • Refund requests submitted more than 7 days after the charge (for initial subscriptions) or outside our exceptional circumstances criteria (for renewals)
  • Accounts found to have violated our Terms of Service
  • Dissatisfaction with the nature of AI-generated content (we encourage using the free trial to evaluate this beforehand)
  • Unused generations — we do not prorate refunds based on how many generations you used
  • Third-party fees (e.g. bKash transaction fees)
  • Promo codes or trial extensions obtained through circumventing our systems

08 Disputes & Chargebacks

We always prefer to resolve issues directly and fairly. If you have a problem with a charge, please contact us before initiating a chargeback with your bank or payment provider. We respond quickly and will work to resolve legitimate issues promptly.

Initiating a chargeback without first contacting us may result in suspension of your account while the dispute is being resolved. Fraudulent chargebacks will be contested.
