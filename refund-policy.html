01 Overview

This Refund Policy applies to all subscriptions purchased through yesteachers.com, operated by YesTeachers (sole proprietor). Payments are processed by Paddle.com Market Limited as our Merchant of Record.

We want you to be satisfied with your purchase. We offer a free trial and a 7-day satisfaction guarantee on first payments so you can evaluate the Service before committing. If something goes wrong on our end, we will make it right.

02 Free Trial

YesTeachers may offer a free trial period to new subscribers via promo code. During the trial:

  • You have full access to all features in your selected plan
  • No payment is charged until the trial period ends
  • You may cancel before the trial ends to avoid any charge
  • Promo codes are single-use and non-transferable
  • Creating multiple accounts to extend a trial violates our Terms of Service

03 7-Day Satisfaction Guarantee

If you subscribe to a paid plan for the first time and are not satisfied, you may request a full refund within 7 days of your initial charge — no questions asked.

To qualify for the 7-day guarantee:

  • Your refund request must be submitted within 7 calendar days of the charge date
  • This applies to your first payment only on a given plan — not renewals
  • Your account must not have violated our Terms of Service

We will process your refund promptly upon verification. Your subscription will be cancelled at the same time.

04 Monthly Renewals

For recurring monthly charges after the first payment, we do not offer refunds for partial months as a standard policy. However, we will consider refund requests in the following exceptional circumstances:

  • Significant technical failures on our side that prevented you from accessing the Service for an extended period (more than 48 hours)
  • Duplicate charges or billing errors caused by our systems
  • A charge processed after a cancellation was properly and verifiably submitted

We do not issue refunds for renewal charges due to: forgetting to cancel, unused generations, dissatisfaction with AI output quality, or change of mind after 7 days of the first payment.

05 Cancellation Policy

You may cancel your subscription at any time. Cancellation:

  • Takes effect at the end of the current billing period
  • Stops any future charges immediately
  • Does not remove your account or data
  • Retains your access to paid features until the period expires

There are no cancellation fees. To cancel, email support@yesteachers.com with your registered email address, or use the account settings in your dashboard.

Cancel before your next billing date and you will not be charged again. We recommend cancelling at least 24 hours before renewal to ensure processing time.

06 How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, follow these steps:

1

Email us

Send an email to support@yesteachers.com with the subject line "Refund Request".

2

Include your details

Provide your registered email address, the date of the charge, the plan name, and a brief reason for your request.

3

We review and respond

We aim to respond to all refund requests within 2 business days and will confirm the outcome via email.

4

Refund issued

Approved refunds are initiated through Paddle back to your original payment method. See processing times below.

07 Refund Processing Times

Once a refund is approved and initiated through Paddle, processing times depend on your payment method:

  • Credit / Debit card: 5–10 business days to appear on your statement (depends on your bank)
  • PayPal: 3–5 business days
  • bKash: 3–7 business days (see bKash section below)

You will receive an email confirmation once the refund has been initiated. If you do not see the refund after the expected time, contact your bank or payment provider before contacting us.

08 Paddle & Merchant of Record

As our Merchant of Record, Paddle.com Market Limited is responsible for processing all payments and refunds on our behalf. When a refund is approved by YesTeachers, it is initiated through Paddle's system.

In some regions, Paddle's own buyer protection policies may apply. You can also contact Paddle directly for billing queries via paddle.com/legal/buyers. However, for the fastest resolution, we recommend contacting YesTeachers directly first.

09 bKash Payments

For users in Bangladesh paying via bKash:

  • Refunds are returned to the original bKash number used for payment
  • Processing typically takes 3–7 business days after approval
  • bKash transaction fees charged at the time of payment are non-refundable as they are collected by bKash, not by YesTeachers
  • We will confirm the refund reference number via email once initiated

If you have a dispute about a bKash charge that you believe was unauthorised, please contact us immediately at support@yesteachers.com.

10 Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will not be issued in the following circumstances:

  • Requests submitted more than 7 days after the initial charge (outside our guarantee window)
  • Renewal charges where cancellation was not submitted before renewal
  • Unused AI generations — we do not prorate based on usage
  • Accounts that have violated our Terms of Service
  • Dissatisfaction with the general quality of AI-generated content (we encourage using the free trial to evaluate the Service)
  • Third-party fees (e.g. bKash transaction fees, bank currency conversion fees)
  • Promo codes or trials obtained through system circumvention

11 Disputes & Chargebacks

We always prefer to resolve issues directly. If you have a concern about a charge, please contact us before initiating a chargeback with your bank or card provider. We respond quickly and will resolve legitimate issues promptly.

Initiating a chargeback without first contacting us may result in temporary suspension of your account while the dispute is investigated. Fraudulent chargebacks will be contested with supporting documentation.

For payment disputes handled through Paddle, their dispute resolution process may also apply. Contact support@yesteachers.com and we will coordinate with Paddle on your behalf.
